Visualization of Topic Modelling Results (LDAvis)¶

*λ: ranking metric: small λ prioritize topic-specific terms; large λ highlight overall important terms, set to 0.5 here.¶

*Circle: topics¶

*Bar: terms belong to topic¶

*A good way to understand the topic is to search the key words with university name in Twitter or Google¶
